Frequently Asked Questions

HaulPro is an all-in-one logistics platform that helps businesses manage transportation, logistics, and shipping operations with ease.

To register, click on the "Sign Up" button on the dashboard and provide your business details. You will receive a confirmation email to activate your account.

After logging in, go to the "Shipping" tab, enter shipment details such as pickup and delivery addresses, and select your preferred vehicle type. Then, confirm and schedule the shipment.

You can contact HaulPro support by clicking the "Contact" button in the dashboard. You will be redirected to Gmail with an automatic email draft addressed to support@haulpro.com.

HaulPro accepts various payment methods, including credit cards, bank transfers, and digital wallets. You can manage your payment options in the "Billing" section of the dashboard.
